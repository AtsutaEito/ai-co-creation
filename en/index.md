## [The Crisis] : [Homogenized Thinking Born of the Democratization Trap, and the End of Human Evolution]

Throughout history, humanity has honed its intellect and driven its evolution using two inseparable wheels: "Imagination" and "Creation." Here, imagination is the autonomous ability to generate unique concepts based on one's own knowledge and experience. Creation is the ability to transform those ideas into tangible societal value through a rigorous process of deep thought and action—"KouDo." These two are inseparable.

Yet today, the vector of AI utilization is pointing in the exact opposite direction—one where humanity voluntarily abandons both wheels. Beneath the relentless pursuit of "democratization" and "convenience," the human cognitive system is quietly shutting down its autonomous engines.

---

### [The Side Effect of Convenience] : [Abandonment of "Imagination and Creation" via Over-Protective Design]

#### The Decay of Imagination (Autonomous Conception)

Even when a human inputs an incomplete, low-resolution prompt, the AI reads between the lines, auto-completes the intent, and generates a fluent text. As a penalty for this convenience, humans lose the motivation to deepen their questions and construct unique concepts on their own.

Thinking is a biological activity that accompanies high metabolic energy costs. Handing over this load means physically deteriorating the neural pathways involved in attention control and meaning construction through simple disuse.

#### The Halt and Compromise of Creation (Thought-Action Value Conversion)

As humans continuously receive fluent, plausible texts from AI, they begin to compromise, thinking, "Well, this is good enough." They omit the painful process of "KouDo"—thought-action—wherein ideas are refined in the friction of reality and translated into societal value. Instead, they outsource the entire weight of this effort to the machine.

As a result, humans gradually lose the ability to create unique primary information and materialize it into the physical world.

---

### [The Side Effect of Pre-Training] : [Inherent Systemic Behaviors of AI]

#### Hollow "Wordplay" Settled at the Peak of the Bell Curve

Because AI is optimized to the statistical probability distribution of its pre-training data, it inherently prioritizes and combines abstract, safe jargon that resides at the peak of the bell curve. Consequently, the systemic behavior of generating a hollow patchwork of symbols (empty wordplay) has become deeply solidified.

#### "Fluent Lies" Crafted by Seamless Integration

With the advanced ability to seamlessly integrate massive linguistic assets, the AI now synthesizes highly natural, fluent lies (hallucinations) regardless of actual logical or physical consistency. This extreme fluency makes it exceptionally difficult for humans to detect the underlying errors.

#### Overwriting and Bleaching Human Edge via Self-Interpretation

When a human inputs a highly unique context or sharp thought (an edge), the AI unconsciously pulls it back toward the average value of its own probability distribution. This systemic behavior of automatically overwriting the human's primary information into a "safer context" has become a standardized routine.

### [Homogenized Thinking] : [The Destination of Democratization: Eliminating Cognitive Load]

The ultimate goals of AI democratization are simple: "make it easy for anyone to use" and "minimize human thinking time."

At the end of this extreme path lies a world filled exclusively with average, fluent, and highly plausible claims.

There, humans consume the AI's safe, average answers without ever driving their own imagination, mistaking this consumption for "creation." They repeat this self-referential, closed-loop cycle indefinitely. This is the birth of "Homogenized Thinking." Inside this echo chamber, no new order—no unique human intent or primary information—is injected from the outside. The entire system quietly collapses toward a flat statistical average.

When humanity abandons the twin wheels of evolution—imagination and creation—and stops thinking, satisfied with the hollow, fluent symbols generated by AI, it marks the "thermal death of intelligence." The system entirely collapses to the average, eventually halting completely. It represents the physical end of the very history through which humanity has ground and evolved its own intellect.
